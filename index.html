Alpha Run Club

This Sunday, somewhere in Pune, people will run together.

Alpha Run Club is where movement dissolves resistance and brings you back to yourself.

We run, train, and gather not to perform, but to feel alive again, together.

We meet every Sunday and run across different parts of Pune. The place changes. The rhythm stays.

Who this is for

  • People who feel life has become too comfortable or numb
  • Those who want to move without being judged or measured
  • Beginners who want honesty, not motivation
  • Runners who care more about presence than pace
